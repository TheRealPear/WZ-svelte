Dear Warzoners,

We're excited to announce a brand-new feature that will elevate your Warzoning experience to new heights: an **Achievement System**! Starting today, your adventures on Warzone will be even more rewarding and dynamic as you embark on quests to unlock prestigious achievements. *Please also kindly note that the system is still in testing phases and minor bugs are expected.*

🔍 **How Does It Work?** 🔍

Simply engage in your usual gameplay activities, and you'll automatically progress towards unlocking achievements. Whether you are a skilled PvP player or an intrepid explorer, there are tons of different kind of achievements tailored to your playstyle. Get ready to explore and unlock over 80+ new and unique achievements.
