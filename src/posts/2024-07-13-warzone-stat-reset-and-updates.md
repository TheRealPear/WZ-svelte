You may have noticed the sudden increase of activity on the server! We noticed too, and the Warzone team has decided there's no better time for a **stat reset!!!** We have some extra changes that we are rolling out at the same time to make this update extra special!

⏰  **When?**

The update will occur on Saturday, July 13, 2024.
We will close the server a few hours before the reset to perform the changes.

📉 **Player Stats Reset**

All your stats will be reset. This includes your level, kills, wins, achievements, and more. **Your ranks & tags will remain.**

💫 **Leveling Progress Change**

Due to much feedback, we are changing the leveling system to the old exponential system where the more you level up, the harder it takes to keep leveling. This means no more people with levels in the high hundreds. We also changed the colors of the levels at every milestone to hopefully be a bit nicer!

📜 **Achievements**

You may have noticed but we recently added an achievement system you can check out with `/achievements`! This will help you keep track of all your cool stats, while increasing your achievement score stat for every achievement you unlock. We will keep working on this system to improve it and bring more features you can interact with.

We hope this encourages you to play, and we hope to see you all soon on the server! A big thanks to ThatOneTqnk, MewTwoKing, Jorgeberrex, Drysu, _Pear, and everyone else on the team who helped make this possible. We look forward to continue to warzone with the community and everyone else involved.
